Deepfake Detection on Social Media: Leveraging Deep Learning and FastText Embeddings for Identifying Machine-Generated Tweets. This project aims to develop effective techniques using deep learning methods combined with FastText embeddings to detect machine-generated content in social media platforms, addressing the challenges posed by misinformation and deepfakes in digital communications.
